Urban Nest Brussels

A high-end living experience: architectural lines, warm materials, soft lighting — a discreet luxury, inspired by the hotel industry.

Habiter Bruxelles autrement

Urban Nest Brussels is an exclusive collection of high-end furnished residences, thoughtfully designed for mid- to long-term stays in Brussels. Bridging the gap between traditional hospitality, often impersonal, and conventional rentals often restrictive, it offers a seamless, refined, and move-in-ready alternative.

Each apartment is conceived as a true urban nest : a functional, warm, and discreet space, ready to be lived in. It is designed for those who come to truly settle in Brussels with comfort, stability, and simplicity.

Fully furnished Including charges 91+ days Premium Brussels locations

The Concept

Urban Nest Brussels presents a refined portfolio of meticulously curated furnished residences, set within Brussels' most prestigious and strategically located neighborhoods. Its ambition is to offer a refined, seamless, and reassuring residential experience, thoughtfully designed for an international, mobile, and discerning clientele.
