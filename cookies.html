Cookie Policy

How and why we use cookies on this website.

Last updated: April 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember your actions and preferences over a period of time, so you don't have to re-enter them whenever you come back to the site.

2. How We Use Cookies

The Urban Nest Brussels website uses cookies in a limited and purposeful way. We do not use cookies for advertising or to track you across other websites. The cookies used on this site fall into the following categories:

Essential cookies Required for the website to function properly. They enable core features such as navigation, page loading, and security. These cannot be disabled. Required
Session cookies Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. They help maintain your browsing session and remember your preferences during a visit. Required
Analytics cookies Optional cookies that allow us to understand how visitors interact with our website (pages visited, time spent, etc.). Data is anonymised and used only to improve the site. Optional

3. Third-Party Cookies

In certain cases, we may use third-party services that set their own cookies. This may include:

  • Google Fonts — used to load typography. Google may set cookies related to font delivery.
  • Analytics services — if analytics are enabled, a third-party provider (e.g. Google Analytics or equivalent) may set anonymised tracking cookies.

We do not use cookies from social media platforms, advertising networks, or retargeting services.

4. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can control and/or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device
  • Block some or all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of this website. For guidance on managing cookies in your specific browser, refer to your browser's help documentation.

5. Consent

When you first visit our website, you may be asked to consent to the use of optional (analytics) cookies. Essential cookies do not require consent as they are strictly necessary for the website to function.

You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings or by contacting us directly.

6. Legal Basis

Our use of cookies is governed by Belgian law implementing EU Directive 2009/136/EC (ePrivacy Directive) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). For more information on how we handle personal data collected via cookies, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. The date of the most recent update is shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to check this page periodically.
